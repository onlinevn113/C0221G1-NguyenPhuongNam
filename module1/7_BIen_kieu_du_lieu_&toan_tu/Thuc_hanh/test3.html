Currency

To offer multiple currencies on your online store, you must first [edit your store's currency formatting](https://docs.haravan.com/support/settings/general/tell-my-script-where-to-find-money-formats).

Even though prices are displayed in different currencies, orders will still be processed in your store's currency.

Use the country's ISO [currency code](http://www.xe.com/iso4217.php). Separate your currency codes with a space.
